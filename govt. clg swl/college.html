Principal Message


On behalf of the staff of Govt. Graduate College Sahiwal, I am pleased to welcome you for selecting this college for academic pursuits. You will find outstanding academic programs and a learning environment that is positioned far above the rest of the colleges in Sahiwal Division and renowned institutions of the Punjab. At GPGCS, students establish the foundation for effective learning, achievement of personal objectives and goals. The College is known as mini University for having nine Postgraduate disciplines in the subjects of Botany, Chemistry, English, Economics, Mathematics, Statistics, Physics, Political Science and Urdu. We started BS-4 year Program in Information Technology, Botany, Chemistry, English, Economics, Mathematics, Physics, Statistics and Political Science in 2010. Our BS classes are going on smoothly and successfully. In future, we have intention to start BS-4 Year Program in Geography, Islamic Studies, Education and Urdu. I believe that our exemplary under-graduate Super section to be continued in 2019 and so on. Our graduation and post-graduation educational programmes are coupled with experienced, highly qualified teaching staff, majestic building and grounds provide the opportunity for academic achievement and expression of veiled potential. I invite you to discover what makes GPGCS an exclusive symbol of learning. GPGCS is renowned for its high level of education and extra-curricular achievements. The students who have passed their examination in different disciplines are holding very high positions in different walks of life giving an honor to the college. Good traditions take years to establish. May Allah bless you and your heart with the light of knowledge so you may be able to play your role in the development of society and prosperity of Pakistan as a true Patriot and a responsible citizen.

Prof. Dr. Mumtaz Ahmad

Principal
Govt. Postgraduate colleges Sahiwal
