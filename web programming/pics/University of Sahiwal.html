Important Notice

1) It has been reported that some students are using the name of University of Sahiwal on Social Media Facebook/Twitter with title official page of the University of Sahiwal. University of Sahiwal has no such page on any Social Media platform. The students are warned to keep themselves away from using such pages and posting any kind of stories/comments. If any student is found creating/misusing the University name on Social Media it will be considered as misconduct and a strict disciplinary action will be taken against the student involved which may include the rustication from the university.
2) The entry of Outsiders is strictly banned inside or around the academic buildings except Administrative Block after clarifying the security checkup at gate. The students who have been called for inquiry in discipline committee ever, no outsider is allowed to enter with them or for meeting with them.
3) The students who have been called for inquiries in discipline committee ever, are not allowed to participate as organizers in any Departmental or University level function or party. The HODs are requested to please consider this matter seriously.
4) Smoking inside or near University premise is strictly prohibited. Strict disciplinary action will be taken against those who violate.
5) Holding any kind of arms, rods, hockey etc. is strictly banned inside or near University premise. Strict disciplinary action will be taken against those who violate.
6) To call on 15 or Police is not allowed without intimation of Security Supervisors, Resident Officer, Incharge Discipline Committee, Department HODs OR Registrar Office.
7) Unless very necessary and needed for a genuine reason, the frequent visits of One Academic Block students into the other block is not allowed.

visit of academic blocks

Workshop on Case-Based Method of Teaching

In line with the vision of worthy vice Chancellor, Prof. Dr. Niaz Ahmad Akhtar, ORIC - University of Sahiwal has collaborated with Sukkur IBA Executive Development Center - EDC of Sukkur IBA University, for the capacity building of the faculty of University of Sahiwal. Hence, a number of faculty members travelled to Sukkur IBA University to participate in a 2-Day Workshop on Case-Based Method of Teaching. The Master Trainers comprising Dr. Muhammad Shaiq, Dr. Obed Rashdi, Mr. Sajjad Mahesar and Dr. Ali Raza jointly conducted their sessions for the diverse group of participants from Sahiwal, Sukkur, Larkana, Hyderabad, and Karachi. In the last session of this workshop, Director EDC, Prof. Dr. Altaf Hussain Samo and Director CRC, Dr. Faiz Muhammad Khawaja distributed certificates among successful candidates. The Vice Chancellor of Sukkur IBA University and Director EDC promised to entertain the request of Director ORIC for an MoU between the two universities during the next visit of the Faculty of University of Sahiwal. The faculty members of University of Sahiwal also visited various sections and departments of Sukkur IBA University and appreciated the state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities available to the faculty and students of Sukkur IBA University.

Vice Chancellor Message

It is my pleasure to welcome you all to the University of Sahiwal. The start of the new academic year is always a special time on our campus, with new students and faculty, new relationships and exciting conversations begin as summer break comes to a close. I encourage incoming students and faculty to get involved in the campus life and contribute to the many activities planned for the upcoming year. New students will take advantage of the new campus facilities on ways of becoming tomorrow leaders. Our challenge is to help generate ideas that will benefit society, and to educate and train people to work in fields where they will be valued both for their specialized knowledge, and for their ability to research, communicate and solve problems. As we look ahead and seek to engage with challenges facing higher education in Pakistan today, we continue to be dedicated to create an environment for our students and faculty to pursue their passions and position our University for future success. The future is now, my fellow students. As has been most famously quoted, “There are no secrets to success. It is the result of preparation, hard work, and learning from failure.” At University of Sahiwal, we have a goal to work in partnership with local and international educational organizations and communities to make a difference at National and International level. I look forward to starting this rich and rewarding journey with each and every one of you. Together will we grow! Wish you all the best.
